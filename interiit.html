Aquatics

Updates

  • The Inter-IIT Aquatics Contingent have left for the event!
  • On the first day of the 34th Inter IIT Aquatics meet, IIT Kharagpur smashed IIT Bombay in Water Polo by 13-2
  • Men's Team 100m Backstroke - Pushpak Roy bagged silver while Gitanshu Bhatia bagged the bronze medal.
  • Men's Team 200m Breaststroke - Indresh bagged silver medal for IIT Kharagpur.
  • Men's Team 100m Breaststroke - Indresh bagged the silver medal.
  • Men's Team 200m Freestyle - Shubham Pandey stood fourth.
  • Men's Team 50m Butterfly - Arpan Dey stood 4th
  • Women's Team 50m Butterfly- Oindrila Saha opened up for kgp by bagging the bronze medal
  • IIT KGP bagged silver medal in Women's 4X50m Freestyle Relay. Relay team comprised of Oindrila Saha, Supriti Sen, Aditi Sen and Kalyani Ingle.
  • Women's 50m Freestyle - Kalyani Ingle stood 4th.
  • Men's 200m Individual Medley - Arpan Dey bagged the silver medal.
  • Men's 50m Backstroke - Arpan Dey and Pushpak Roy made IIT KGP proud by bagging the Gold and Bronze medal respectively.
  • IIT KGP bagged silver medal in Men's 4X100 Medley Relay.
  • Men's Team 200m Backstroke - Gitanshu Bhatia and Pushpak Roy bagged the gold and silver medal respectively.
  • Men's Team 50m Breaststroke - Indresh wins one more silver medal making the count to three.
  • Men's Water Polo - IIT KGP defeated IIT Guwahati by 13-2 in semi- final to face IIT Kanpur in final on 7/10/18.
